# Story
## Chapter One
### Apples
Terry begins his shift for the day as a street-sweeper. He hears shuffling nearby, and looks up to see a particularly striking young woman picking apples in the nearby orchard. The woman suddenly swings around and the two exchange a brief glance. They both quickly avert their eyes; they know it's illegal to look at the other sex. But Terry looks on in admiration of her efficiency. The woman's figure is slender and she stands on tall legs as she continues her path, clearly much better at her job than the other women. After a while of continued sweeping, the mysterious woman had disappeared out of his view.

### Fire
After completing his work for the day, all inhabitants of Kalusna are summoned to the town square where an execution by fire is being prepared. A man named Menfra is burned at the stake, accused of corrupting the people with forbidden knowledge. The onlookers shout "Traitor!" as they pelt him with stones. As the man nears his end, he stares into Terry's eyes, as if bestowing a gift onto him. Despite being ostensibly evil, Terry can't bring himself to hurt the man, questioning whether Menfra really did anything wrong. In Terry's mind, the man was a saint.

### Night Terrors
Terry has trouble sleeping as visions of Saint Menfra appear in his mind. Terry becomes overwhelmed and decides to go for a walk in the middle of the night. He evades the night watchmen as he wanders about the village, eventually straying into the apple orchard where he had seen the mysterious woman earlier, and then into a field on the very outskirts of the village. Suddenly, a creature lunges at him from the forest, and he has no choice but to fight. After fending off the creature, Terry silently realizes he has much more strength than he thought. He finds a strange, rusted-over metal plate in the grass. Upon lifting it up and seeing what's underneath, he can hardly believe his eyes. Not quite confident enough to explore alone, he goes back in search of a friend to go exploring with him.

### Remnant
Terry runs back to the village and awakes his best friend, Dex. Dex is hesitant and insists on staying in bed to avoid punishment, but Terry eventually convinces him. The two set off to find the entrance again. Once there, the two use an old ladder to descend into a large, foreign structure, unlike anything they had ever seen in Kalusna. Strange rectangular panels have been affixed into the ceiling every few meters, while metal rods bolted to the ground extend forward with seemingly no end. Dex is quite baffled by the sight, but the structure piques Terry's interest. He insists that the pair stay inside the structure and explore until the morning, ignoring whatever dangers may lie ahead. The two explore for hours. Terry is fascinated by the Oldworld artifacts, while Dex remains skeptical as everything they encounter violates what he knows. After some time, they find the entrance and return to their beds, just in time for their work in the morning.

## Chapter Two
### Viola
After Terry begins his work, he sees the same mysterious woman picking apples nearby. The two exchange some brief words, and the woman reveals her name: Viola. The two decide to speak again the next day. 

# People
## Terry
(initial): Terry is a 21-year old citizen of Kalusna. At age 16, when all men in the village are given their Assignment, he is given the task of street-sweeper despite his wishes to work in the Scripture Facility. 

## Mysterious Woman -> Viola
(initial): This mysterious apple-picker intrigues Terry with her exceptional features and the efficiency with which she works.
(+ CH1->Remnant): After exchanging a few words, Terry becomes enamored and decides he will tell her the next day about what he had discovered in the Ancient Vestige.

## Saint Menfra
(after CH1->Fire): Menfra is a citizen of Kalusna about whom not much is known. He was executed by The Heroes on the charge of distributing forbidden knowledge, to which Terry gave him the moniker 'Saint Menfra.'

# Places
## Kalusna
(initial): Kalusna is a rainy, cold village where dark clouds often dominate the sky. To its inhabitants, it is the only place on Earth where humans are permitted to live: tales of beasts in the outlying forests as well as the widespread fear of capital punishment have kept its inhabitants from exploring beyond the outskirts.

## Ancient Vestige -> Kaeri Transit Sytem
(CH1->Remnant): This structure was apparently built by the Ancients. Little is known about it.
(after discovering its name): This structure was built by the Kaeri a thousand years ago. It was an underground railway transit system. After the Reform, the system fell into disuse and it was ordered that all entrances and exits be sealed off.
